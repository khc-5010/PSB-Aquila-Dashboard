## Executive Summary

Virginia's plastics manufacturing landscape is geographically dispersed across the Shenandoah Valley/I-81 corridor, Blue Ridge cities (Roanoke, Lynchburg), and Hampton Roads — with zero injection molding in Northern Virginia. Of ~25 unique CWP companies, only 3 proved to be viable Alliance prospects after investigation (P1 Technologies/Plastics One, Blue Ridge Industries, and Monoflo International). Beach Mold & Tool Virginia (Emporia) is confirmed dissolved December 2021. M&H Plastics is now Berry Global. CAAA and Arya Plastic are ghost entries with no verifiable plastics operations.
