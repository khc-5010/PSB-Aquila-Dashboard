## Colorado Market Observations

1. **Front Range medical molding corridor:** 8-10 companies with cleanrooms/ISO 13485 from CO Springs to Fort Collins.
2. **Longmont is densest cluster:** PTA, AIM, WP Mfg, Byers, RapidPro all within miles.
3. **PE activity accelerating:** Three acquisitions 2022-2025 (Tenere, AIM, Injectech). AIM and Injectech in optimal window.
4. **RJG penetration shallow:** Only Entegris (captive) and LTM Plastics confirmed. Greenfield opportunity for most molders.
5. **Defense/aerospace thread:** Multiple ITAR-registered molders + only AS9100 molder in state (V&S Molding).
6. **Independent ownership dominates:** PTA, LTM, Caroba, Sovereign, WP, Eldon James, Byers, Columbine, Moldrite — most accessible prospects.
7. **Mold making mostly integrated:** Few standalone tool shops. Most toolmaking embedded within molding operations.
