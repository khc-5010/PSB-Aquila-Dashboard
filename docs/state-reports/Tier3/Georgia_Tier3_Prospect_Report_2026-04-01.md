Top 12 targets received full deep profiles.
**CWP Cross-References Found:** No confirmed Penn State Behrend alumni connections. Strongest CWP relationships: Piolax (5 contacts, VP Engineering), Dixien LLC (2 contacts, CEO + Leader New Tools).
**Cross-Tier Conflicts Identified:** evco plastics (Calhoun GA plant — already scored in WI Tier 2, 4 CWP contacts at WI HQ; GA Program Manager contact is additive intelligence only), Flambeau Inc. (Madison GA plant — already scored in WI Tier 2, 4 CWP contacts at WI HQ; GA Engineering Manager + Maintenance Manager contacts are additive intelligence only).

**Tier 3 threshold applied:** Georgia companies required multiple strong readiness signals to justify geographic distance from Erie, PA. GA yielded one confirmed RJG signal (Accurate Plastics & Mold — discovered via beyond-CWP sweep, not in PSB database), multiple PE acquisition windows, and a dense Peachtree City cluster with medical-automotive convergence. The beyond-CWP discovery sweep matched the Minnesota benchmark, surfacing 6 HP targets including the state's only confirmed RJG signal.

**Beyond-CWP methodology note:** Georgia is the second state (after Minnesota) where supplementary web research was conducted to identify strong prospects with no PSB training history. Non-CWP companies are flagged throughout this report. They get scored on the same rubric but require cold outreach rather than leveraging existing PSB contacts.

**RJG Training Center proximity note:** RJG Inc. operates a Southeast Regional Training Center in Woodstock, GA (opened October 2013, 8,000 sq ft, up to 7 machines, classroom for 24 students). This proximity reduces barriers for Georgia molders to engage with RJG ecosystem, making the "RJG-trained but no eDart hardware" gap especially actionable for the alliance.
