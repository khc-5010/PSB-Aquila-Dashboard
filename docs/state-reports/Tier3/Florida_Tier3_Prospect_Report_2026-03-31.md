**Search Focus:** Converters and Mold/Tool Makers in Florida — Tampa Bay / Clearwater corridor, Orlando / Central FL corridor, South Florida / Miami corridor, and secondary clusters (Sarasota, DeLand, Space Coast, West Palm Beach)

The Tampa Bay medical corridor produced the densest cluster of in-scope converters and mold makers.
