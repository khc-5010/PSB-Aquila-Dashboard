## BEYOND-CWP SWEEP RESULTS

The following entities were identified and confirmed as NOT injection molders:

No other plastics manufacturing entities of any kind were identified in Alaska. The state's manufacturing sector is dominated by seafood processing, oil/gas field equipment, and timber products.

---

## NOTES

- Alaska is among the most geographically distant U.S. states from PSB (~4,000+ miles). Even if a molder existed, the Tier 3 bar would require extraordinary signals to justify engagement at this distance.
- Alaska's manufacturing economy is resource-extraction oriented (oil, gas, fish, timber) with essentially zero consumer or industrial products manufacturing.
- Resin supply chains do not extend to Alaska — raw materials would need to ship from Gulf Coast or Pacific Northwest ports at prohibitive cost, and finished parts would need to ship out via the same routes.
