**PE consolidation pattern:** Four PE-backed platforms operate in AZ: TruArc/Aptyx (2022), Lee Equity/Westfall (2017), Sunningdale/Moldworx (2020+2024), Water Street/Viant (exited via closure). Active PE interest in AZ medical molding.

**CWP database inadequacy for AZ:** Only 3 of ~25 CWP companies are viable prospects. The beyond-CWP sweep was decisive — surfacing 7 of 10 pipeline companies. This reinforces the pattern seen in smaller Tier 3 states where CWP coverage inversely correlates with pipeline quality.

**Medical device corridor:** Phoenix metro (Tempe/Scottsdale/Chandler) hosts a medical device contract molding cluster: ICON, Aptyx, Harris Precision Mold, Moldworx. The West Pharma CMP operation anchors the high end. ISO 13485 certification is common.

**MedPlast/Viant resolution:** Definitively answered — MedPlast was NOT acquired by Spectrum Plastics Group. MedPlast → Viant Medical (2018, Water Street/JLL), Tempe closed 2019. Spectrum Plastics Group → DuPont (2023). Completely separate ownership chains. Spectrum's AZ facilities (Tucson, Nogales) are silicone/extrusion, not thermoplastic injection molding.
