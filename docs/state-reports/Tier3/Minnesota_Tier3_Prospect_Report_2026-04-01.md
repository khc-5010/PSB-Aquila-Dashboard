# ALLIANCE PROSPECT REPORT — Tier 3 Minnesota
## April 1, 2026

**Search Focus:** Converters and Mold/Tool Makers in Minnesota — Medical Device Alley supply chain (Minneapolis-St. Paul metro), Anoka-Rogers-Forest Lake-Ramsey tooling corridor, Greater Minnesota manufacturing centers (Rush City, Alexandria, Mora), and near-border MSP-market companies (Baldwin WI, Hudson WI)
**Source:** Michelle Hortmann's CWP database (9,750 rows → 120 MN plastics-type rows → 96 unique companies after dedup → 49 researched) PLUS beyond-CWP web discovery sweep (16 additional companies)
**Companies Profiled (Deep Research):** 44 total in pipeline — 18 High Priority, 12 Qualified, 14 Watch List. 25 excludes removed (13 OEM, 11 non-manufacturer, 1 CWP ghost). Top 12 targets received full deep profiles.
**CWP Cross-References Found:** No confirmed Penn State Behrend alumni connections. Strongest CWP relationship: General Pattern (5 contacts including President).

**Tier 3 threshold applied:** Minnesota companies required multiple strong readiness signals to justify geographic distance from Erie, PA. MN yielded the highest concentration of scientific molding practitioners of any Tier 3 state and 3 confirmed RJG/eDart users. The Medical Device Alley ecosystem produces an unusually dense cluster of ISO 13485-certified molders with scientific molding capabilities. This is the first state where the beyond-CWP discovery sweep was applied — it surfaced 6 High Priority targets that do not appear in the PSB database, including the #1 RJG signal in the pipeline (Mold-Tech).

**Beyond-CWP methodology note:** All prior state analyses (CA, TX, FL, NC, and all Tier 1/2 states) were limited to CWP-sourced companies. Minnesota is the first state where supplementary web research was conducted to identify strong prospects with no PSB training history. Non-CWP companies are flagged throughout this report. They get scored on the same rubric but require cold outreach rather than leveraging existing PSB contacts.
